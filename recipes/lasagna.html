Lasagna Recipe

Description

Ingredients

  • 12 whole wheat lasagna noodles
  • 1 pound ground beef
  • 2 cloves garlic, chopped
  • 1 teaspoon dried oregano, or to taste

Steps

  1. Preheat the oven to 360 degrees
  2. bring large pot of light salted water to boil. Add lasagna noodles and cook for 10 minutes or until al dente; drain
  3. place ground beef, seasonings, in large skillet over medium heat
